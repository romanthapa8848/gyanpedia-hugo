About Gyanpedia

Welcome to Gyanpedia, a premier free educational encyclopedia designed to empower students across Nepal and India. Our mission is simple: to provide accessible, high-quality educational resources to students from all walks of life.

What is Gyanpedia?

Gyanpedia is a comprehensive online platform offering a wide range of educational materials in one place. Whether you're a school student preparing for exams or a college student looking for subject-specific guides, we have everything you need to succeed. From detailed notes and curriculum guides to old exam questions and study tips, Gyanpedia is your go-to resource for educational support.

Our Features

  • Free Educational Notes: Access detailed and easy-to-understand notes for all subjects, aligned with the Nepalese and Indian syllabi.
  • Syllabus Breakdown: Find the complete syllabus for School (Class 1-12), University, and Competitive Exams.
  • Study Guides: In-depth guides and tutorials to help you understand complex concepts.
  • Old Question Papers: Access past exam questions from various boards and universities to help you prepare for exams.
  • Interactive Content: Engage with quizzes, mock tests, and more to assess your learning and track your progress.
