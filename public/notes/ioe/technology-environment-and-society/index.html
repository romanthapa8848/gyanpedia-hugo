Technology, Environment and Society

Course Code CE 753
Theory Assessment Mark: 10; Final Exam: 40 Marks (Duration: 50 Marks)
Practical
Total Marks 50 Marks

Course Objectives

  • To provide knowledge of the environment, technology, and its impact on society.
  • To understand global, national, and local environmental issues.
  • To explore the challenges posed by the information society.
